We face situations every day that require us to make choices.

Every choice has a consequence. You choose your choice but you don't choose the consequence of your choice.

Have you chosen life?

Have you chosen Jesus?

Our Vision

Habakkuk 2:14 summarises our vision; For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the LORD, as the waters cover the sea.

Our Mission

Jesus Over Everything is a movement geared towards making Jesus famous. We long to see those who have not yet known Jesus Christ experience Him and to help Christians grow in their knowledge of Jesus, our Lord.
