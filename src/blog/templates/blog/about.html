About us at Jesus over everything

About Us

Nowadays, the media is being used to fulfill every other agenda apart from the agenda of God. Satan is, of course relentless in his plan to bring at least a filthy content to our faces every day, especially to the Christian so that it becomes a seed of corruption which grows to a mighty tree in the heart.

Jesus Over Everything is a global community that stands as part of the army of Christ in media. We intend to join the rest of Jesus' soldiers to fill this space with godly content that reveals more of Jesus to you.

This is a meeting point between you and Jesus.

You will know Him more before leaving.

You are welcome

Our Vision

Habakkuk 2:14 summarises our vision; For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the LORD, as the waters cover the sea.

Our Mission

Jesus Over Everything is a movement geared towards making Jesus famous. We long to see those who have not yet known Jesus Christ experience Him and to help Christians grow in their knowledge of Jesus, our Lord.

Meet the founder

Dr Seyi Erinle is a medical doctor. He is passionate about Jesus and the expansion of His Kingdom.

His greatest joy is seeing people discover their purpose in God, helping them pursue consistent growth and lead great lives.
